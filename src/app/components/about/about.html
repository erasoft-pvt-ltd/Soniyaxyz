About Our Company

Strengthening Your Workforce

Cosmos Group is a dynamic organization providing a wide array of innovative and unique solutions in a number of fields. The organization was established in 1985 by Late S.N. Trivedi, an ex-Indian Air Force. Operating for more than three decades, the organization has evolved in itself, as well as in its services. Presently, Cosmos Group is spearheaded under the leadership of Dr. Sameer Trivedi, son of Late S.N. Trivedi.

Cosmos Group, an ISO 9001-2015 certified company is a highly professional, employee-friendly, and compliant organization that deals with the most important asset for any organization – Human resources. Cosmos is India’s leading Integrated Security & Manpower Solution Organization, its headquarter is based in Gandhinagar, Gujarat, India and has its branches and operation all over the country.

Dr. Balram Kumar

COO

I'm Dr. Balram Kumar, and my career path has been a fascinating journey. I began as a doctor in the medical field, later completing MBA in Business Marketing. In 2018 my professional journey continued at Dixon Technologies as a Manager.

During my tenure, I witnessed firsthand the struggles of youth and the pressing issue of unemployment. This experience sparked a desire to make a meaningful impact. I started exploring teams that shared my vision of empowering youth and providing guidance.

That's when I met the co-founders of Sarv Siddh. I was impressed by their mission and passion. As I joined the team, I realized that this was the perfect opportunity to turn my ambitions into reality.
